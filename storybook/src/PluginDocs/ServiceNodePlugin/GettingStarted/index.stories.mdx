# Getting Started

##

This plugin provides adds an **ExpressJS** micro-service into your gluestack project.

This allows you to focus on writing your **custom logic** and building your service without having to worry about setting up the basic functionality.
You have the flexibility to customize the service according to your needs and preferences.
